DADDY SHRIMP

A free underwater arena game · early access

Daddy Shrimp is a free, browser-based multiplayer game. You're a glowing little shrimp in a neon ocean: eat, grow, and out-swim everyone else in fast 2-minute rounds. Hand-drawn art, real-time arenas, no downloads, no real money — just fun.

How to play

  • Move: on a phone, touch and drag to steer with the joystick. On a computer, your shrimp follows your mouse.
  • Grow: swim over glowing tokens to eat them and get bigger.
  • Chomp: you can eat shrimp smaller than you — but bigger ones can eat you, so pick your fights.
  • Watch the hazards: jellyfish, sea urchins, and anemones sting; rocks and coral block your path. When you're small, hide inside an anemone to stay safe.
  • Survive the round: last until the 2-minute timer ends to bank your tokens. Get eaten and you respawn small to try again.
  • Sign in (optional): add your email to save your stats across devices and start earning Founder Credits.

What are Founder Credits?

Founder Credits are our way of recognizing early players. You earn them every round you play — and unlike your in-round score, they're never lost when you get eaten. They have no cash value; they're a loyalty reward that will unlock cosmetic perks as the game grows. Get in early, and you build a balance that means something later.
